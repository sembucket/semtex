Maps for book Pioneering Irrigation in Australia to 1920

Required information

  • Major rivers (and creeks, if appropriate), labelled. Minor waterways can be unlabelled.
  • Major water storages, preferably natural (including swamps), labelled.
  • Major towns, labelled.
  • Major geographic features (e.g. mountain ranges, districts) labelled.

When considering labelling, give preference to those which appear in book's index.

Maps required

  1. Murray-Darling system. This will appear as a two-page spread, but desired as a single map.
  2. South Australia/Adelaide. Mainly rivers and towns that do not appear in the Murray-Darling map. Single page.
  3. Tasmania. Single page. 
For all these maps, we have suggested protoypes. The Murray-Darling map prototype needs amendment to include Macquarie and Namoi rivers, and if possible will show also the Hunter, Nepean and Hawkesbury rivers. If really necessary we can accommodate another map just for NSW.

Output files

Encapsulated Postscript, without preview. Preferably as vector format (i.e. not raster, unless very high resolution). Black and white. Helvetica fonts.
